QUESTIONS

What is an SEO Audit for websites?

An SEO site audit is essential to any business that wants their website to rank better in the search engines. When done correctly, an SEO audit will give you technical insights into your website and its webpages, and provide best practice recommendations on what can be done to improve its performance from an SEO perspective.

An SEO audit normally checks the following parameters: 
1) Indexing and crawlability issues;
2) Website speed and rendering issues;
3) On-page technical analysis; and
4) keyword and content analysis.

Getting all of these things right is crucial for a better SEO score of your website.

How long does an SEO Audit take?

Depending on the size and complexity of your website and its webpages, a basic SEO audit is performed relatively quickly with the assistance of our advanced SEO audit tool. Larger sites with more pages to analyse can take several hours to perform a deeper scan. Note: our free SEO audits are limited to smaller less complex websites. For larger audits you can tell us about your requirements by calling 1300 707 660.

How do you carry out the SEO Audit?

Performing an SEO audit is completed with our in-house SEO audit software. We don’t require log-in details to your website, but view only access to your Google Search Console account will help us provide more comprehensive findings. Our SEO audit tool checks your entire website and crawls its HTML source code to provide technical recommendations on what can be improved. This report is presented to you in an easy to read PDF document so you can action the recommend improvements step-by-step.
