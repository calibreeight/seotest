FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Local SEO?

LOCAL SEO:
Local SEO (Search Engine Optimisation) is the process of optimising your online business towards local customers. It helps businesses promote their goods and services at the exact time the customer is searching for it.

GOOGLE MY BUSINESS:
Google My Business (GMB) is an online marketing tool that helps businesses get found in Google Maps, Local Finder, Google’s Local Pack, and organic search engine results. It displays useful information such as opening hours, images, location, phone number, e-mail address and more.

Why is Local SEO important?

FOR CUSTOMERS:
Local SEO helps customers find products and services close to them. Rather than searching the web through countless websites to see which are in their area, it displays relevant results immediately.

FOR BUSINESSES:
Local SEO helps businesses show up as a relevant result in search engines, which can increase organic traffic and attract customers. Since these leads are from people searching for your product or service, it often results in high conversion rates.

How do you improve Local SEO results?

6 STEPS:
1. Create & Optimise a Google My Business account
2. Get 5 star reviews
3. Keyword research for search engines & GMB
4. Target most relevant keywords
5. Optimise your website for mobile
6. Strengthen your website with link building
