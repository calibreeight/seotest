Your personal information that may be held by SEO Advantage include your name, address, telephone number, email address, website address and other information.

Directly from you when you supply us with information over the phone, through email or when you submit an enquiry via our website (e.g. contact forms)

Third parties such as your representatives or our related companies

Your personal information may be used to:

- Verify your identity
