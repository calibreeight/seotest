FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What exactly does a copywriter do?

A copywriter creates clear and compelling copy which is used to advertise promotional material. This can include websites, emails, landing pages, product descriptions, product descriptions, newsletters and more. The text written is known as “copy”. It’s purpose is to get the reader to take action and engage with your brand or business. Copywriting is best used in conjunction with Search Engine Optimisation to convert new traffic.

What exactly does a content writer do?

Content writing is creative online content, such as blog posts, e-books, podcast scripts, video scripts, sales copy, etc. Content writing focuses on being well-written, creative, researched and most importantly, targeted towards the intended audience.

What is the difference between copywriting and content writing?

The main difference between copywriting and content writing is the motive behind it. Copywriting is used for promotional advertising, while content writing is meant to engage the reader. Both are used with the intentions of selling products or services, but copywriting is a more direct approach while content writing is an indirect approach.
